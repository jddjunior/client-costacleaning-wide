Clear space & minimum size
Keep clear space equal to the height of the wave on all sides. Never render the mark below 24px or the full lockup below 120px wide.
Do & don't
Use cobalt on light, white on cobalt or ink.
Keep the wave and wordmark proportions locked.
Don't recolor, rotate, or add effects to the wave.
Don't place the cobalt mark on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
